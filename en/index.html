About

Located in Taxco’s historic downtown, Hotel Santa Prisca preserves colonial charm in every detail. Its architecture, courtyards, and balconies invite you to enjoy a comfortable, authentic stay.

  • Central location, just minutes from the main attractions.
  • Colonial-style spaces with daily housekeeping.
  • Warm service and a family-friendly atmosphere.
